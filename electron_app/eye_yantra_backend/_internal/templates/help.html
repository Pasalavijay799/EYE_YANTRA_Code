Common Issues & Solutions

⚠️ Can't connect to device?

  • Make sure Bluetooth or Wi-Fi is enabled.
  • Keep the diagnostic device powered on and in range.
  • Try refreshing the device list or restarting the connection.

🎥 Camera not showing preview?

  • Grant browser camera permissions.
  • Ensure no other app is using the webcam.
  • Reload the page and retry.

📄 Reports not generating?

  • Make sure all test stages are completed.
  • Try refreshing the report page.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. Who is this system for? ▼
Designed primarily for ophthalmologists, optometrists, and vision researchers, but can also be used in remote screening centers.
Q2. Is this a medical-grade diagnostic tool? ▼
It is a research-based tool intended to aid clinical judgment. Final diagnosis should be confirmed by certified professionals.
Q3. What patient data is stored? ▼
Only the patient's name and test data are temporarily held for report generation. No sensitive data is stored permanently unless enabled by the clinician.
