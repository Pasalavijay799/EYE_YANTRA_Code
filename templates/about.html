About Strabismus Detection Suite

AI-Powered Eye Care Diagnostics

Introduction

Strabismus Detection Suite is a pioneering AI-powered web-based tool designed to assist healthcare professionals in the early detection and assessment of strabismus (commonly known as squint) in patients. This system integrates advanced computer vision techniques with proven clinical tests to deliver accurate, efficient, and non-invasive diagnostics.

Project Overview

Developed in collaboration with faculty from the Indian Institute of Technology Tirupati, this application supports three core diagnostic assessments:

🔹 Preliminary Eye Check

Initial assessment to identify potential vision irregularities and eye alignment issues

🔹 Hirschberg Test

Corneal Light Reflex Test for precise measurement of eye deviation angles

🔹 9-Gaze Diagnostic Test

Comprehensive eye movement analysis across nine directional positions

The interface is built for ease-of-use in clinical settings, research trials, and remote screenings.
